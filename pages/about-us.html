About Us

Your story can make a difference.

Our Vision

We empower and equip diverse creators so that all voices are heard.

Your story deserves to be on the shelf, in the hands of readers and in the minds of those willing to dive into a different perspective.


Our Story

Born out of the many travesties of 2020, Diversify Print desires to see more diverse voices in print, change the way the average bookshelf looks and launch writers into becoming successful authors.

We are here to serve the voices unheard and to empower each individual with the knowledge and skills to be successful in sharing their stories.

Diversify Print offers personalized plans, writing coaching and marketing training to help writers develop what they want their publishing journey to reflect. We are committed to empowering and developing new writers, unique perspectives and most of all, diverse voices.


About Our Founder

Katie Long, Founder and President of Diversify Print, hopes to join you on your publishing journey. She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Professional Writing and over seven years of publishing experience. She will walk alongside you through each step of the life of your manuscript, and throughout your progression as a writer.

Katie started her professional work as a children’s librarian. Soon after that, her publishing career began in editorial. She then moved into marketing and acquisitions, and she is currently leading a team at a large publishing house.

Originally from Indiana, Katie now lives in Colorado with her husband, son and two cats, trying to get as close to the mountains as possible.
